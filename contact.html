What to include

Share details about your room (size, ceiling height), the mood you're aiming for (cozy, focused, energizing), and any current lighting problems (too dim, harsh shadows, uneven coverage). The more context you provide, the better we can guide you.

A simple, personalized recommendation covering brightness levels, color warmth (cool white vs. warm white), and ideal placement for your fixtures. We'll suggest products that fit your space and budget, with clear explanations for every choice.
